Best Scuba Diving in Camiguin Island

Camiguin Island is one of the most beautiful and uncrowded scuba diving destinations in the Philippines. Surrounded by volcanic reefs, healthy corals and rich marine life, Camiguin offers excellent visibility and warm water all year round.

Blue Season Camiguin is a professional PADI scuba diving center located in Anito, Mambajao. We provide PADI Open Water courses, Advanced courses, fun dives, snorkeling trips and turtle diving at Mantigue Island.

Mantigue Island Turtle Diving

Mantigue Island is the most popular dive site in Camiguin. Divers can often encounter large green turtles, schools of jackfish, reef fish and colorful nudibranchs. It is suitable for both beginner and certified divers.
