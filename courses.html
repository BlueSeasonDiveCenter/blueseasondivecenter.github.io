Most Popular Course

PADI Open Water Diver

⏱ 3–4 days 🌊 Max depth: 18m 🏅 Worldwide certification

The PADI Open Water Diver course is the world's most popular scuba certification. Complete your course in the warm, clear waters of Camiguin and earn a certification that lets you dive anywhere in the world down to 18 meters.

  • PADI eLearning materials (study on-line before arrival)
  • 5 Confined water skills sessions
  • 4 open water training dives
  • All diving equipment included
  • PADI certification eCard & registration
  • Small group: max 4 students per instructor

Level Up

PADI Advanced Open Water

⏱ 2–3 days 🌊 Max depth: 30m 🏅 Advanced certification

Ready to go deeper? The Advanced Open Water course expands your diving skills with 5 adventure dives. Required courses include: deep dive and underwater navigation. Recommended elective courses include: Peak Performance Buoyancy, night dive, drift dive, or fish identification.

  • 5 adventure dives
  • All diving equipment included
  • PADI certification eCard & registration
