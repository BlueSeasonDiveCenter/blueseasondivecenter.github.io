🌊 Limited-Time Event · June 2026

Crown-of-Thorns Starfish Clean-Up

Small actions. Big impact. Together, let's restore Camiguin's reefs — one starfish at a time.

This June 2026, every diver joining a Fun Dive with Blue Season Dive Center at White Island, Camiguin is invited to take part in our Crown-of-Thorns Starfish Clean-Up.

Crown-of-thorns starfish are one of the major threats to coral reefs. By helping remove them, you directly contribute to the health and recovery of our marine ecosystem — and you're rewarded for it.

🎁 Earn Discounts While Saving Our Reefs
