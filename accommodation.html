Stay right on the water at Blue Season's cozy dive resort in Camiguin. Roll out of bed, grab breakfast, and walk straight to the dive boat. Your Camiguin diving basecamp.

About the Resort

Quiet Comfort by the Sea

Blue Season Camiguin's resort is located on the peaceful shores of Anito, Mambajao — far from the noise but close to all the best dive sites. Our rooms are designed for divers who want comfort without pretension: clean, air-conditioned, and just steps from the ocean.

The resort sits on a quiet stretch of coastline with its own beach access, a freshwater swimming pool, and a restaurant serving a mix of Filipino and international cuisine. Whether you're spending a few days diving or a full week unwinding, this is your home on Camiguin.
